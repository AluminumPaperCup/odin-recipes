Homemade Popcorn Recipe

Homemade Popcorn made with a Whirly Pop, coconut oil, and flavacol that will surely fire up those movies nights. With the proper kernels and the right toppings, your movie night can be taken to the next level by following this

Ingredients

  • Kernels [any kind]
  • Flavacol [0.5 teaspoon]
  • Coconut Oil [10 mL]
  • KERNEL'S Topping [your choice]
  • Whirly Pop popcorn Popper
  • Clarified Butter [as much as you want]

Steps

  1. 1. Setup the Whirly Pop on stove and put on low to medium heat
  2. 2. Add 3 to 4 tablespoons of coconut oil
  3. 3. Measure 3/4 cup of kernels
  4. 4. Pour half a teaspoon of flavacol and stir using Whirly Pop lid
  5. 5. Put one kernel of popcorn in Whirly Pop and close lid and wait for it too pop
  6. 6. Add the rest of the popcorn kernels in and reduce heat, stir slowly until popping slows
  7. 7. Transfer freshly popped popcorn into bowl, and add your melted clarified butter
  8. 8. [OPTIONAL] Add your desired choice of KERNEL's Topping flavour and shake into bowl!
  9. 9. Enjoy!
